The person behind the plan

Hello, I'm Natsayi.

A Registered Holistic Naturopathic Nutritionist and Certified GAPS Coach, with a special interest in brain nutrition for children — and adults — living with complex autism and ADHD.

Why I do this

It started with my own son

My journey into naturopathic nutrition began at my own kitchen table — searching for answers, feeling overwhelmed, and hoping for something that actually worked. I saw first-hand how the right nutrition can change a child's physical health, and with it their mood, focus, and connection.

That experience became my life's work. My approach is grounded in empathy, science, and deep respect for each child's individuality — because no two children, and no two families, are the same.

Timing matters

Two windows worth taking

The developing brain has seasons of rapid change. Nourishing it well during these windows can make a lasting difference.

Before age 9

The years when grey matter is developing most intensively — a powerful time to support learning, focus, and regulation through nutrition.

Up to age 19

White matter continues to mature into the late teens, so there is still real opportunity to strengthen connection and resilience.
