Before you ask

Questions answered

I don't have a medical background — will I understand this?+

Yes. Everything is explained in plain, practical language — what to do and why, not a biochemistry lecture.

I've tried programmes before and didn't see results. How is this different?+

Most programmes hand you a protocol and disappear. This one is clinical nutrition plus fortnightly accountability with direct access to Natsayi, so when something isn't working, you're not troubleshooting it alone.

My child is older — is it too late?+

No. The brain can build new connections at any age. The focus is on giving it what it needs to do that, whatever age your child is now.

Are results guaranteed?+

No, and anyone who tells you otherwise is lying to you. What we can tell you is that this approach is grounded in clinical experience and research, progress is tracked through ATEC scoring, and the testimonials above are real families who agreed to put their names to them.
