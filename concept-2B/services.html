Not guesswork

Functional testing comes first

Before we recommend anything, we look at what your child's biology is actually telling us. Functional lab testing helps identify the gut, metabolic, and nutritional imbalances that are often driving behaviour, sleep, and communication difficulties — so the plan that follows is built around your child, not a template.

  • Gut and digestive function markers
  • Metabolic and mineral balance
  • Nutritional deficiencies and absorption
  • Root-cause drivers behind behaviour and sleep
