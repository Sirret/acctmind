Using naturopathic and functional-medicine-informed principles, we take a holistic view of your child's biology — looking beneath the surface at gut health, metabolic imbalances, nutritional deficiencies, targeted supplementation, and environmental factors that influence behaviour, learning, sleep, and overall brain function.

  • Environmental exposures — mould, heavy metals, and toxin load
